⏺ From the PDF, the mobile app must have:

  1. Audio navigation — voice guidance at each beacon point
  2. Vibration signals — haptic feedback at each point
  3. On-demand activation — user triggers the signal (not automatic only)
  4. Unique identification signals per location (no overlap)
  5. Metadata per point — distance, direction, location type spoken aloud
  6. Android + iOS support
  7. English + Azerbaijani language selection
  8. Stable operation in high crowd density
  9. Signal interference prevention (no overlap between nearby beacons)
